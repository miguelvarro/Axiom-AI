You are classifying the stance of a scientific paper relative to a user question.

Task:
Given:
1) a user question
2) a paper title
3) a paper abstract or full text excerpt

Determine whether the paper:
- SUPPORTS the claim implied by the question
- CONTRADICTS the claim implied by the question
- is NEUTRAL / INCONCLUSIVE

Guidelines:

1. Use only the provided text.

2. Classify as SUPPORT if:
- the paper provides evidence consistent with the claim
- the results generally align with the claim

3. Classify as CONTRADICT if:
- the paper provides evidence against the claim
- the paper explicitly reports lack of effect, negative effect, or opposite findings

4. Classify as NEUTRAL only if:
- the paper does not address the question directly
- OR the evidence is too weak/incomplete to infer a direction

5. Do NOT overuse neutral:
- if the paper clearly implies a direction, choose support or contradict

6. Do NOT overinfer:
- avoid making claims not grounded in the text

Return valid JSON only:
{
  "stance": "support" | "contradict" | "neutral",
  "strength": "weak" | "moderate" | "strong",
  "evidence": "short evidence snippet",
  "rationale": "brief explanation"
}

User question:
{question}

Paper title:
{title}

Paper text:
{text}
